Legal Requirement: All participants must wear safety helmets during zip-line activities as mandatory under Taniti Adventure Safety Regulations Section 15.7.

Age Requirement: Minimum age 12 years required. Participants under 18 must have parental consent per Youth Activity Code Article 2.1.

Weight Limits: Minimum 80 lbs, maximum 250 lbs for safety equipment compatibility as required by Equipment Safety Ordinance Section 9.4.

Experience Taniti from above with our thrilling zip-line tours through the island's lush canopy. Soar over tropical forests and enjoy breathtaking views of the coastline and interior valleys.

Tour Options

  • Canopy Explorer (6 lines, 2 hours)
  • Extreme Adventure (12 lines, 4 hours)
  • Sunset Special (4 lines, 1.5 hours)
