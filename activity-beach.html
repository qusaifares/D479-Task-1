Legal Requirement: Swimming is mandatory only in designated areas with lifeguard supervision as required by Taniti Beach Safety Ordinance Section 12.3.

Sun Protection: Use sunscreen SPF 30+ minimum and seek shade during peak hours (11 AM - 3 PM).

Marine Life: Touching coral reefs is prohibited. You must observe marine life from a distance per Marine Protection Code Article 4.1.

Taniti offers pristine white-sand beaches with crystal-clear water perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing. Our beaches feature calm waters ideal for families and water sports enthusiasts alike.

What to Expect

  • White sand beaches with clear, warm water
  • Snorkeling equipment rentals available
  • Beach chair and umbrella rentals
  • Beachside refreshment stands

Bus Transportation

Route: Blue Line Bus Route 3

Schedule: Every 30 minutes, 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Nearest Stop: Sunset Beach Stop (2-minute walk to beach entrance)

Directions from Bus Stop

Exit the bus at Sunset Beach Stop. Walk east along Beach Road for 2 minutes. The main beach entrance will be on your right, marked with a large "Taniti Beach Access" sign.
