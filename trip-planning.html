What are the alcohol service hours?

Alcohol is served in restaurants and bars from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM Monday through Saturday. Sunday service begins at 1:00 PM. Some resorts may have extended hours for guests.

Are there any local regulations I should know about?

Yes, helmet laws apply to all zip-lining and bike rental activities. Swimming is only permitted in designated areas with lifeguard supervision. Fishing requires a local permit available at the marina.

What's the local currency and payment methods?

Taniti uses the US Dollar. Credit cards are widely accepted, but it's recommended to carry some cash for local markets and small vendors.

What should I pack for activities?

Bring sunscreen (SPF 30+), insect repellent, comfortable walking shoes, and a reusable water bottle. For hiking, sturdy boots are recommended. Beach activities require swimwear and sun protection.
