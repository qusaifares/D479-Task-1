Hiking & Nature Trails

‚ö†Ô∏è Hiking Legal Requirements & Safety

Legal Requirement: All hikers must carry emergency contact information and inform park authorities of hiking plans as mandatory under Taniti Trail Safety Regulations Section 8.2.

Required Gear: Sturdy hiking boots, water bottle (minimum 1 liter), sun protection, and insect repellent are mandatory.

Weather Advisory: Checking weather conditions before hiking is required. Trails may close during heavy rain per Emergency Closure Code Article 3.4.

Explore scenic mountain trails and lush jungle paths across Taniti. Our hiking trails offer stunning views of the island's diverse landscapes, from coastal vistas to tropical rainforest canopies.

Trail Options

  • Coastal Ridge Trail (Easy, 2 hours)
  • Rainforest Loop (Moderate, 3 hours)
  • Summit Peak Trail (Challenging, 5 hours)
  • Waterfall Discovery Path (Easy, 1.5 hours)

Bus Transportation

Route: Green Line Bus Route 7

Schedule: Every 45 minutes, 6:30 AM - 6:30 PM

Nearest Stop: Mountain Trail Head Stop (5-minute walk to main trailhead)

Directions from Bus Stop

Exit the bus at Mountain Trail Head Stop. Follow the marked trail signs north for 5 minutes along the gravel path. The main trailhead visitor center will be on your left.
