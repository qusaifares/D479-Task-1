üìÖ Important Dining Information

Holiday Closures

Most restaurants close on major holidays including New Year's Day, Independence Day, and Christmas Day. Many family-owned establishments also close on local festival days.

Alcohol Service Hours

Alcohol is served Monday-Saturday: 11 AM - 11 PM, Sunday: 1 PM - 11 PM. Some resort restaurants may have extended hours for guests.

Reservations

Reservations recommended for dinner at upscale restaurants, especially during peak tourist season (December-April).

Local Specialties

Don't miss: Fresh coconut water, tropical fruit smoothies, grilled fish with island spices, and traditional breadfruit dishes.
